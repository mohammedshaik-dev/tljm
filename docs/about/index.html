The Triplicane Labbai Jamaath Masjid

The Triplicane Labbai Jamaath Masjid (Tamil: ) is located in the Triplicane neighborhood of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1889, the mosque has been a significant religious and cultural landmark.

  • Historical Significance

    The mosque has a rich history dating back to the 1870s, founded by Tamil-speaking Muslims who came to Chennai for business in lungis, dry fish, beedi, and other trades.

  • Community and Culture

    This mosque serves primarily Tamil Muslims, with Friday sermons conducted in Tamil. It has been a place of worship and gathering for local and distant worshippers alike.

Over the years, the mosque has been an integral part of the community, and major renovations are currently being planned to preserve and enhance its historical structure.
